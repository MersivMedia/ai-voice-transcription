Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

ResumeGlow ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information to provide our service:

  • Resume Content: The bullet points, job titles, and industry information you enter to improve your resume. This data is processed in real-time and is NOT stored on our servers after processing.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data such as page views, device type, and general location (country/region) to improve our service.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for essential site functionality and advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the resume improvement service
  • To generate the requested document downloads
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements

Data Retention

We do not store your resume content. The text you enter is sent to our AI processing service, used to generate improvements, and immediately discarded. We do not maintain any database of user resume content.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: To process and improve your resume bullets. Their privacy policy is available at openai.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. Learn more at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Vercel: For hosting our website. Their privacy policy is available at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect about you
  • Request deletion of any data (though we don't store resume content)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Since we don't store your resume content, there is no database of personal resume information that could be breached.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@resumeglow.com
